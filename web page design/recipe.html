How to Make Cannoli Dip!

This creamy dip tastes just like cannoli filling! Serve with broken up waffle cones to act as the shell and to scoop up this delicious dip and enjoy!

Ingredients

  • 2 cups ricotta cheese
  • 1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese
  • 1 1/2 cups confectioners' sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 cup miniture chocolate chips

Directions

  1. Beat ricotta cheese and cream cheese together in a bowl until smooth
  2. Add sugar and vanilla extract to the mixture slowly
  3. Once the sugar and vanilla extract are mixed in fully, fold chocolate chips in the mixture
  4. Cover bowl in plastic wrap and refrigerate until chilled, at least 10 minutes or more
  5. While the dip is chilling, break up waffle cones in bite sized pieces to use as a scoop for the dip

Prep time: 10 minutes, Ready in: 20 minutes

Serve cold with waffle cone bits. Enjoy!
